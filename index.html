About

For as long as I can remember, art has been a part of my life. At an early age, I would watch Bob Ross on public television and try to replicate his paintings with my watercolors. It was not long after that endeavor, I picked up a pair of drum sticks, which lead me further on my journey of self-expression. I’ve been blessed over the past twenty-five years, to have lead various creative-art programs. During that time, I gained experience in media and graphic design. Recently, I have made another shift in my creative journey - training to become a UX/UI front-end web developer.


When I’m not designing websites, you will find me with my self-professed family of “nerds” who love all things tech and art. We live in Austin, which proudly proclaims itself as the “music capital of the world”. This is fitting since we are a family of musicians who loves to perform in our community. It’s in this atmosphere of creative energy that I draw inspiration for my work.


My design aesthetic is minimalist. I’m passionate about creating beautiful, responsive websites that work on different size devices. I strive to design clear and seamless websites that provide a pleasant and successful web experience for the end-user. Thank you for taking the time to check out my website. I would love the opportunity to bring your web design ideas to life!
